DIY Cherry Blossom Dreams

Follow this step-by-step tutorial to create a delicate cherry blossom nail art design, perfect for spring or any special occasion.

Pink

Floral

Spring

signal_cellular_altDifficulty:Intermediate
timerTime:~45 mins

Instructions & Tools

1. Prepare Your Base

Apply a base coat, then two thin coats of light pink polish to all nails. Let dry completely.

Ballet Slippers Polish

2. Outline Petals

Using white nail art polish and a detailer brush, create small, delicate petal shapes for the cherry blossoms. Focus on creating an organic, clustered look.

Alpine Snow Art Polish Detailer Brush No. 5

3. Add Pink Detail

Once the white is dry, use a slightly darker pink or a diluted primary pink to add subtle shading and depth to the petals. Don't worry about perfection; natural variations look beautiful.

Ballet Slippers Polish Detailer Brush No. 5

4. Create Flower Centers

With a dotting tool and a darker, contrasting color (e.g., brown or a deep pink), add tiny dots in the center of each blossom to create stamens. Allow to dry.

Detailer Brush No. 5 (use back end for dotting)

5. Seal with Top Coat

Finish with a matte top coat to give your cherry blossoms a soft, realistic finish. This protects your design and adds a professional touch.

Matte Top Coat
